## Course Description

This course introduces the core frameworks of marketing management — segmentation, targeting, positioning, the 4 Ps, and brand strategy — through a mix of case discussion, applied exercises, and a semester-long team project. Students leave able to evaluate a marketing strategy, design a market entry plan, and defend recommendations with data.

## Required Readings

- Kotler & Keller, *Marketing Management*, 16th edition (textbook)
- HBR case packet (distributed via Canvas, week 2)
- Selected articles posted weekly to Canvas
